KHARAN DESERT

The Kharan Desert is a vast desert located in the southwestern region of Pakistan, within Balochistan province. Known for its rugged terrain, sandy plains, and sparse vegetation, it experiences extreme temperatures with very little rainfall, making it one of the harshest environments in the country. Despite its arid conditions, the desert is home to wildlife adapted to the harsh climate, and it supports nomadic communities who rely on livestock for their livelihood, reflecting both the natural and cultural uniqueness of this region.
